With this app, Show the last price, last quantity of product were supplied to customer before when create new quote or sale order.

Show the last price, last quantity of product were supplied to customer before when create new quote or sale order.

List features:

1. Show last purchase info of product that was supplied to customer before.

2. Only show on quote. NOT on sale order.

3. the last purchase will be changed automatically when customer is changed.

When you see "Quote: S00046". That mean, last time this product was quoted to customer on 09/12/2021 and customer hasn't bought this product before.
When you see "Order: S00044". That mean, last time this product was supplied to customer on 09/12/2021.
